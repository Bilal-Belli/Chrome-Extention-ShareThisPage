Privacy & Terms

Privacy Policy

Share This Page Pro operates entirely locally. We do not collect, store, or transmit any data. Your browsing history, URL shares, and searches never leave your device.

Permissions Used

  • activeTab: To retrieve the URL and title of the active tab when you click to share it.
  • clipboardWrite: To copy the shareable link and title to your clipboard.
  • storage: To save your settings and preferences locally.
